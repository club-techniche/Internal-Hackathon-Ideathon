Domain:- Healthcare, Artificial Intelligence (AI in Medical Imaging), Bio-Technology

Title:- AI-Driven Disease Diagnosis

Background

Early detection is crucial for treating serious diseases like cancer and diabetic retinopathy (a retinal issue). However, specialist resources (radiologists, ophthalmologists) are scarce. AI-driven image recognition models can analyze medical scans (X-rays, fundus photos) to quickly spot minute anomalies, democratizing early screening.

Importance

It enables mass-scale, early screening in remote or resource-limited areas (Telemedicine), significantly increasing patient survival rates and preventing blindness. It also reduces the workload on specialists, allowing them to focus on complex cases.
