6. "Eco-Lens" - AI-Powered Sustainable Consumerism

Background

Consumers increasingly want to make environmentally friendly purchases, but determining the true sustainability of a product is often very difficult. Many products use misleading eco-friendly claims, a practice known as “greenwashing,” and complex supply chains make it hard to verify whether a product is genuinely sustainable. Additionally, the lack of transparent, easily accessible data turns ethical shopping into a time-consuming and frustrating process.

Problem Statement

Current shopping methods do not provide consumers with real-time, reliable information about a product's environmental impact, leaving them unsure whether their choices are truly sustainable. This gap makes it challenging for people to support ethical brands and make environmentally conscious decisions effectively.

Goal

The goal is to create a mobile application that empowers consumers to make instant, informed, and sustainable choices while shopping. The app should provide clear information about a product's environmental footprint, materials, and supply chain practices, helping users identify genuinely eco-friendly products. By reducing confusion caused by greenwashing and lack of transparency, the app will make ethical shopping faster, simpler, and more reliable, encouraging responsible consumption and promoting sustainability.
