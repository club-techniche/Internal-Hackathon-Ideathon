Title:- Child-Safe Internet Router Add-on

Background

The digital world exposes children to inappropriate content (violence, adult material) and online risks (cyberbullying, addiction). While many routers have built-in controls, non-technical parents find them confusing, buried in technical settings, and ineffective for setting simple screen time or content rules across multiple devices.

Importance

The solution provides an intuitive, centralized tool for managing an entire home network, ensuring a safer digital environment for children. It promotes digital well-being by setting screen time limits and helps parents maintain a balanced, open discussion with their children about internet safety.

Challenges

Teams must design an interface that is extremely simple for non-tech users (e.g., a "plug-and-play" app setup), ensure the filters are difficult for tech-savvy children to bypass (e.g., via VPNs or DNS changes), and manage privacy concerns around monitoring a child's browsing history.
