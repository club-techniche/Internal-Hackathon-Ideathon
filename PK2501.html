Inclusive Education Tool for Differently-Abled Students

Background

Education is a fundamental right, but many differently-abled students face barriers in accessing quality learning resources. Students with visual, hearing, mobility, or cognitive challenges often struggle in traditional classrooms because existing teaching tools are not designed with inclusivity in mind. Inclusive education tools can bridge this gap by leveraging technology to provide equal opportunities for learning, active participation, and academic growth for all students.

Problem Statement

Differently-abled students often face barriers in accessing quality learning materials due to the lack of inclusive teaching tools. Traditional classrooms and digital resources are rarely designed to accommodate visual, hearing, mobility, or cognitive challenges, limiting equal participation and academic growth.

Goal

Design a software-based solution that ensures learning materials are accessible and engaging for differently-abled students.
