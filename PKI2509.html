Domain:- Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Media Literacy

Title:- Fake News Detector

Background

The rapid spread of misinformation ("fake news") on social media and the internet has severe societal impacts (political, health). Manual fact-checking cannot keep pace. An AI-powered tool can automate credibility assessment by analyzing text features (sentiment, source, linguistic patterns) and cross-referencing public fact-checking APIs.

Importance

It empowers the average internet user to make informed decisions by providing a quick, non-technical credibility score. It helps slow the viral spread of misinformation and reinforces the importance of media literacy in the digital age.

Challenges

The core challenge is Algorithmic Accuracy (distinguishing sophisticated fake news from satire or opinion), the Transparency of the score (explaining why a source is rated low), and the Reliance on External APIs which might be language-dependent, biased, or unavailable for real-time fact-checking.
