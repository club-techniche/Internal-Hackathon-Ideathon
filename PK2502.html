2. Smart Home Automation for Energy Efficiency and Safety

Background

Many homes are not energy-efficient and often lack systems to detect hazards, leading to unnecessary electricity consumption and potential accidents.

Problem Statement

Traditional homes waste electricity because lights, fans, and appliances are left on unnecessarily, and they lack hazard detection systems for fire, gas leaks, or water overflow.

Goal

Design a smart home automation system that automatically manages energy usage by controlling lights, fans, and appliances, while also detecting hazards like fire, gas leaks, or water overflow and alerting the homeowner.
